Work Holidays

The average amount of holidays a company provides in the United States is 6 per year. You may use the average or change it to the amount of holidays your company provides. If you're not sure then you can leave it as 6 or set it to whatever you think is best.

You should not include any pto, sick, vaction, or any other type of time off that is not a holiday.
