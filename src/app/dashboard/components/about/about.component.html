About

Welcome to our remote work savings calculator! At Remote Work Savings, we are dedicated to helping you unlock the financial benefits of remote work. Our platform leverages data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration EIA.gov to provide you with accurate and up-to-date calculations on the potential savings you can achieve by working remotely.

Remote work has become an increasingly popular choice for individuals and businesses alike. With the flexibility to work from anywhere, you can save on commuting costs, reduce your carbon footprint, and enjoy a better work-life balance. Our calculator takes into account various factors such as average commuting distance, fuel prices, food/beverage costs, child care, and more to give you an estimate of your potential savings.

We pride ourselves on the reliability and accuracy of our calculations. By sourcing data directly from EIA.gov, a trusted authority in energy statistics, we ensure that our results are based on the most reliable information available. We believe in transparency, and that's why we invite you to check out our GitHub page, where you can explore the source code behind our calculator.
